The gap is infrastructure — which requires zero design work to solve. Here's the honest map of where we stand and what to build next.

The GPT audit is directionally right, but wrong on timing
Don't build the backend before you have users. The audit assumes you have a dev team and validated demand. You don't yet — and that's normal at this stage. The right order is: (1) deploy what you have and get 100 real users, (2) talk to them and find out what they'd pay for, (3) build only the infrastructure that unlocks that value. The 90-day roadmap above reflects this — Phase 1 is deploy and gather signal, Phase 2+ is build based on what users tell you. A backend no one uses is wasted engineering.
